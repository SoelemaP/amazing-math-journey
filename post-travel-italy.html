My Travel Adventure to Italy! ✈️

Ciao, lovely readers! 🌟 Buckle up, because I’m taking you on my whirlwind journey from Suriname to Italy! 🌍✈️

I set off for the airport on Sunday, September 15, at 1 PM Suriname time, feeling a mix of excitement and nerves. The flight took off at 4:30 PM, and my first stop was Amsterdam. After about 8.5 of crossing oceans and time zones, I landed at Schiphol airport. 🚀

Navigating Schiphol? Oh boy, it was like a mini marathon! 🏃🏾‍♀️This airport is HUGE, and walking from the arrival gate to the departure gate took up more than half of my 2.5-hour layover. To add to the fun, there was a little delay at passport control because the officer was not familiar with my type of visa. He had to call in reinforcements—his colleague—for a second look! 🤔 He also told me, that he had never heard of a Surinamese person traveling to Italy for study before. Guess I’m making history! 😄

After that we got that straightened out, I hopped on a 1 hour 45-minute flight to Milan. Now, Milan's airport was another adventure! I was extremely tired and sleep-deprived, and on top of that, I had to walk a total of 1 hour from the arrival gate to the departure gate. 😩 Language barriers didn’t help, especially when there was an announcement in Italian. I didn’t understand a word but saw everyone getting up and walking away. I thought to myself, "Well, if you’re all leaving, then so am I!" 😂 I quickly got up, checked the monitors, and found out about a last-minute gate change. Lucky for me, it wasn't too far from where I was. Phew! 🎉

Finally, after a short flight to Lamezia Terme, I landed at 4 PM and made my way to the university by 5 PM local time. What a journey! 🌟

If you want to hear more about my adventures, make sure to subscribe for updates and check out my Instagram for pictures and videos! Can’t wait to share more! 📲

Every journey has its challenges, but those challenges shape us into who we are meant to be.
Arrivederci!👋
