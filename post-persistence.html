✨ Persistence Pays Off: My Journey to a Scholarship 🎓🌟

Hello readers, I want to share a personal story that I hope will inspire and encourage you to never give up on your dreams. 🌠

In 2020, during the last year of my bachelor's program, I applied for a scholarship to KU Leuven in Belgium. I got admitted but didn't secure the scholarship. Then the pandemic hit, delaying my studies by a year. I reapplied the following year but faced the same result – admission without a scholarship. Financial constraints forced me to withdraw my application. 😔

Undeterred, I applied to Twente University in the Netherlands at the beginning of 2023. Again, I was admitted but didn't receive the scholarship. Coming from a poor family, with my mom working as a cleaner, affording this dream was impossible without financial aid. Discouraged and disappointed, I decided to give up on pursuing a master’s degree, as no such programs are available in my home country, Suriname. 💔

Then, in February 2024, a visiting professor from UVA told me about a scholarship at the University of Calabria (UNICAL) in Italy after being introduced through an ex-professor from my bachelor's program. Curious and hopeful, I researched the master's program in Mathematics and contacted a current scholarship recipient. He was able to provide me with valuable insights regarding his experience at UNICAL. Feeling inspired, I decided to apply. 💡✨

In mid-May, I received the incredible news: I had been awarded the scholarship! My whole body shook with excitement and disbelief. 🎉🙌

This journey has taught me that setbacks are a part of life, and they make us stronger. I believe I didn't get those earlier scholarships because they weren't meant for me. If you're facing rejections and obstacles, remember – persistence pays off. Keep pushing forward, and the right opportunity will come your way. 💪🚀

Stay curious, keep pushing boundaries, and never stop dreaming or give up on your dreams.
Soelema
